## Development

Interference detection between autonomous optimizers is the primary active development area. The detection pipeline — watermark injection, spectral analysis, permutation testing — is implemented and validated on linear coupling channels. Nonlinear channels remain an active research frontier.

- [Bench Scenarios](bench_scenarios.md) — available bench scenarios with detection status per scenario
- [Detection Capabilities](detection_capabilities.md) — channel taxonomy and honest boundaries of what works and what doesn't yet
- [Open Research](open_research.md) — active research directions for unsolved channel types and future capabilities
